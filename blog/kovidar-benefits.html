Kovidar Benefits (Kachnar)

Kovidar, commonly known as Kachnar, is a traditional Ayurvedic tree used in formulations like Kanchanar Guggulu.

1. Thyroid Support

Traditionally used for glandular balance.

2. Women's Health

Often referenced in Ayurvedic approaches to hormonal balance.

3. Detox & Metabolism

Supports metabolic function in traditional systems.

Scientific Perspective

Modern research is still emerging. Kovidar should not replace medical treatment.
